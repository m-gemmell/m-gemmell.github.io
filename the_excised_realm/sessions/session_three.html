Session Three

Plan

  • Seasonal time skip choice
    • Pilgrimage, Courtesy, or service (pg. 17)
  • Mention that the remaining sun worshippers were sent to the Tangled seer to be his caretakers
  • Feast of the stars
    • Night of honours, unions, and contests
    • Takes place outside at the town centre
    • Currently the walls of the forge are fully taken down so they can be rebuilt
    • Starts with Mr. Something reciting their Terminator poem
    • The green eyed seer gifts the deep purple metal claw severed from its steed/tower to be used as the reliquary
      • Standing on its cut base with three equidistant claws reaching up gives the form of a massive metal flower.
    • The feast begins at sunset with the Green Eyed seer leaving to the skies
    • Ask Foxtrot if they want a new knight
      • If so The Leaf knight will challenge them to a duel to claim rulership of Fabriknock
      • Otherwise will still challenge for a duel but just for glory
  • Ongoing effect
    • The sun is dim and the 4 green stars are bright.
    • A poor harvest will occur and the knights controlling the realm will be unhappy with the Tithe.
    • The Emerald lights adore the Company referring the company as “The four that brought the fourth”
    • Other ruling knights and some vassals are not happy due to the company increasing the power of the emerald lights religion.
      • Some will refer to them derisively as “The Terminators”

Time skip to Feast of the Stars

Between seasons tasks

  • The Silk Knight: They fixed the boat/fishing house in 4-11 helping provide enough food for Fabriknock during the poor harvest season
    • They now know how to very effectively make nets
  • The Story Knight: They made a pilgrimage to Bachordil
    • They learnt about the deep purple metal pyramid there and how The Emerald Lights use it as a church
  • The Violet Knight: Pilgrimage to Klaegcasleen
    • Learnt about the The Citadel and how it is used for public events
  • The Salt Knight: Courtesy to the Tangled Seer
    • Brought the remnants of the sun religion as its caretakers and helped rebuild its sanctum
    • The seers are pleased with The Salt Knight

The Feast of Stars

  • Main things listed in plan above
  • The Violet Knight is challenged to a duel by The Leaf Knight
    • The winner will gain a boon from the other
    • The Leaf Knight’s condition is that they can only fight with sticks
    • The Violet Knight’s condition is they can only fight in the dark
    • The Violet Knight using their UV vision beats the Leaf Knight and gouges out one of her eyes (mortal wound)
    • The town leave her for dead celebrating The Violet Knight but The Salt Knight and The Silk Knight patch up the now one eyed Leaf Knight
  • The Violet Knight’s boon? To become ruler of Fabriknock and so The Leaf Knight joins the company

Leaving town

  • As the company leaves town, being seen off by The Violet Knight they encounter The Bone Knight
  • She is escorting potters to make a new clay brick forge building
  • She quickly greets the company, shocked to see The Leaf knight has joined them and Teh Violet Knight has taken over Fabriknock
  • The Knights meet Bray & Hygo, master and apprentice potter come to fix the forge building.
    • Bray asks the Knights to investigate the cliffs at 10-9 to see if their are deposits of blue clay
