The Aurora’s Avarice

Chapter 5 “Base camp”

Point of no return

A large parking lot at the base of the mountain. This is where all cars and other out of town transport stop. Two of Skyfry’s Ski lifts largest vehicles are generally always waiting here, a E-minbus and an E-van. One of the Bjorkson brothers is in a small cabin waiting to use one or the other.

This is the first place the hunter’s will arrive and so the first person they meet will be a Bjorkson brother.

As they travel up the winding road for an hour the first thing they will see of the town of Skifry is its town symbol on a welcome to sign.

If the hunters try to return to base camp before solving the mystery they will find the minibus & van have “broken down”. Unfortunately the normal Ski lift taxis can’t make it down the steep road.
