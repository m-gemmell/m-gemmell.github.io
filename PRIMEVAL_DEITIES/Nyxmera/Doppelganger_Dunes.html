Doppelgänger Dunes

A Nyxmera company that lives and works in deserts. They love temperature extremes of day & night. Additionally, they adore the clearness of the air so they can observe the day and night skies.

The company and its employees carry out various jobs in deserts including:

  • Mercenary work
  • Sand mining
  • Guides
  • Farming & herding
  • Trading

Apart from work they also partake in the following past times:

  • Make Sand Mandalas. In this case it is to worship the extremes of building and destruction for Nyxmera.
  • Play Mancala. They play this game with a glass board and coloured small bones of BONEHEADS.
  • Drink Marsala. This is a fortified wine, normally from the Italian region surrounding the city of Marsala.

SKYBOUND DOPPELGANGER

3 HP | 0 ARMOR | 1D6 appear | EYES : +2 | JAWS : 0 | LIMBS : +1 | NERVES : -1

  • Laser pistol (1D6 DAMAGE)
  • Aeolian whistle
  • Water cooled/heated pilot jumpsuit and pilot’s helmet with full face visor
  • Pilots an Armored powered parachute
  • Eyesight of an eagle
  • Think themselves superior to the SANDBOUND DOPPELGANGERS
  • Listless on the ground only feeling truly at home in the skies above deserts

AEOLIAN CHARMER: They are masters at taming and herding flying desert creatures. To carry this out they carve aeolian whistles, whistles played by the wind. These can be attached to their powered parachutes for constant noise or spun around on a piece of string for better control.

Armored powered parachute

6 HP, 1 ARMOR with mounted laser gun (1D6+1 DAMAGE)

A powered parachute is an aircraft with a parafoil with motor and wheels.

The Armored powered parachutes holds one pilot and no passengers. It is has a reinforced bottom where a compact mounted laser gun is also situated. This gun is designed to take out ground based targets and is controlled by the pilot through a controls alongside the vehicle controls.

SANDBOUND DOPPELGANGER

4 HP | 1 ARMOR | 2D6 appear | EYES : +1 | JAWS : +1 | LIMBS : 0 | NERVES : -1

  • Laser rifle (1D6+1 DAMAGE)
  • Combat shovel (3 DAMAGE)
  • Night vision capable monocular
  • Helmet with full face an scalp reflective visor
  • Special glass plated armor that can reflect and absorb infra-red for heat modulation (1 ARMOR)
  • Envy the SKYBOUND DOPPELGANGERS
  • Readily willing to gamble over a game of Mancala

SAND PERMEATION: These sand troopers are able to dive feet first into sand as if it was air. This effect only lasts a couple seconds after which they will rise back to the surface in an arc motion. This allows them to easily outmanoeuvre foes during close range combats.
