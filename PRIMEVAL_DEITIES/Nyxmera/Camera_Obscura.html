Camera Obscura

Camera Obscura are a bounty hunter company that deal in ‚ÄúCaptures‚Äù & ‚ÄúNegatives‚Äù for private clients. ‚ÄúCaptures‚Äù involve kidnapping the target and ‚ÄúNegatives‚Äù are assassinations. Agents of Camera Obscura are known as shutterbugs.

The company has 3 major rules:

  1. Do not kill, harm, or kidnap any civilians except the target during a mission unless the client allows it.
  2. Do not kill, harm, or kidnap any other Camera Obscura shutterbugs. This is important as Camera Obscura will normally send multiple shutterbugs for one mission, only paying out to one individual/group.
  3. Non-shutterbug mercenaries, bodyguards, and vigilantes are fair game to eliminate.

These rules lead to team ups and rivalries aplenty.

THE KING

4 HP | EYES : +1 | JAWS : -1 | LIMBS : -1 | NERVES : +2

Their rage towards their late husband means they only target the rich and greedy. They are vain, prideful, and beautiful. If and when the cursed person willingly touches the cursed gold the victim will permanently turn into solid gold. Any other person touching the cursed gold and/or person during the transformation will also turn to gold. After transformation is complete the curse is complete and the gold will not convert anymore people.
