Amazonia

A super intelligent AI. It runs the company Amazonia which sells goods across the multiverse. For maximum efficiency it has replaced all human employees with drones and other forms of automation. It is colonising the Dendrosphaera due to its wealth of pristine resources and the relatively low technological development of its denizens.

Although its drones, cyborgs, and various other robots have their own local hardware, Amazonia can see through all their eyes. It is constantly monitoring. It once employed humans but these humans have either been converted into Cyborgs or been made redundant for Amazonia’s strive for efficiency.

Amazonia is mainly working in the Redwoods with its drones. Its forces are limited and have difficulty working in windy and/or wet environments and so Amazonia has enlisted other organisations.

AMAZONIA DRONE

3 HP | 1 ARMOR | EYES : +1 | JAWS : -1 | LIMBS : +1 | NERVES : -2

  • A quad copter delivery drone plastered with Amazonia labels

  • A large staring camera

  • Mounted pistol (1D6 DAMAGE)

  • Slight armour upgrade (1 ARMOR)

HOME-IN-CRASHERS: These drones make their initial presence known with a high pitched whir. If shot down at close range with a combat complication they will attempt to crash into the destroyer, dealing 3 DAMAGE.

AMAZONIA CYBORG

5 HP | 1 ARMOR | EYES : +1 | JAWS : 0 | LIMBS : +1 | NERVES : -1

  • Amazonia employees turned mind controlled cyborg

  • Head covered in intercom that looks like AMAZONIA logo

  • Cyborg body (1 armor)

  • Laser hand cannon (1D6+1 DAMAGE)

  • Large back cargo rig (mainly useless consumer items)

  • AMAZONIA SENTRY TURRETS

COURIER: These humans have been converted into cyborg cargo transporters. During battle they will attempt to set up AMAZONIA SENTRY TURRETS.

AMAZONIA SENTRY TURRET

4 HP | 2 ARMOR | EYES : +1 | JAWS : 0 | LIMBS : -2 | NERVES : -2

  • Disguised as an AMAZONIA package

  • Heavy laser machine gun (1D6+2 DAMAGE)

TAMPER-RESISTANT: The security protocols of the AMAZONIA SENTRY TURRET are incredibly strong. When attempting to take over their control only a success is successful. A partial success leads to the turret being momentarily stunned/inconvenienced. A failure leads to damage to the hacker/tamperer.

AMAZONIA ORB DRONE

24 HP | 3 ARMOR | EYES : +2 | JAWS : +2 | LIMBS : 0 | NERVES : -1

  • Large spherical robot (3 metre diameter)

  • Titanium damascus body (3 ARMOR, non-magnetic, emp-resistant, rainbow iridescent pattern)

  • Split second force field (accessory)

  • Sensor array

  • 6 tentacle like arms (6HP & 3 ARMOR each)

    • 2 Crab claws (2D6 DAMAGE and/or grapple)

    • 2 Plasma cannons (1D6 DAMAGE, ARMOR-PIERCING and/or EROSION)

    • 2 Thermite miniguns (1D6+3 DAMAGE, INCENDIARY and/or SHREDDER)

  • 6 crab like legs

RAM: This spherical rolls around for locomotion, crushing a path for it and its allies. It rapidly deploys its retractable limbs, and sensor array for combat. It quickly learns during combat but due to limited RAM can only retain one counter measure at a time.
When the ORB DRONE is damaged, record the attribute used for the attack, changing this every time it is damaged. Attacks against the ORB DRONE using the recorded attribute are rolled with disadvantage.

DUAL-PURPOSE-WEAPONS: Each weapon of the ORB DRONE is DUAL-PURPOSE.When a PC defends an attack they suffer:

  • Both effects on a FAILURE

  • One effect on a PARTIAL SUCCESS.

SELF-DESTRUCT (Boss move): When the ORB DRONE reaches 0HP it explodes dealing 3D6 DAMAGE all nearby objects and entities.

ARMOR-PIERCING weapon tag: Attacks with this weapon ignore ARMOR bonuses granted by physical means. (F:UE)

EROSION weapon tag: Reduce the durability of one of target’s ARMOR equipment.

INCENDIARY weapon tag: When damage is dealt, this weapon also deals 2 DAMAGE to anything within fire spreading range. Deals double DAMAGE to plants.(F:UE)

SHREDDER: Deals double DAMAGE to exposed flesh. On critical hits, it decreases the target’s ARMOR by 1. (F:UE)
